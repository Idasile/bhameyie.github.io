About this blog

The title "47 ronin" is in reference to the japanese tale of the same name. As the story relates to 47 ronin (i.e. leaderless samurai) who coordinated their effort to achieve a common goal, I thought it appropriate to use as the title of a blog about distributed systems (decoupled and sometime leaderless 'entities'/actors/services which work together to achieve their intended purpose).
